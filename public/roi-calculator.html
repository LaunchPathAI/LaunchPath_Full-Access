Disclaimers & Formula Disclosure

Disclaimer: This ROI projection is a mathematical model based on user-provided inputs and historical institutional averages. It is intended for strategic comparison only and does not guarantee future earnings, job placement, or specific loan interest rates. Actual results vary based on individual performance, market shifts, and specific financial aid packages.

ROI Formula: 5-Year Career Earnings (net of 22% estimated tax) minus Total Cost Burden. Total Cost Calculation: (Annual Net Cost × 4) + (Total Principal × 68% financed share × 6.39% loan rate × 5-year interest period). Education Return Score: Composite score based on brand strength, projected year-5 salary, and total cost burden. Elite institutions carry accelerated early-career salary growth (8–14% annually, tapering) vs. standard baseline (6–8% annually, tapering), reflecting stronger recruiting pipelines and alumni networks.
