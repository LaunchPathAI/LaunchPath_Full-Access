How to interpret your scores
Use these ranges to understand where your essay stands right now.
90–100 = Standout
Top-tier, distinctive, and likely memorable to admissions readers.
80–89 = Strong
Competitive essay with only minor improvements still needed.
70–79 = Solid
Good foundation, but needs sharper insight and differentiation.
60–69 = Developing
Shows promise but still lacks distinction.
Risk levels
Low = no major red flags · Medium = some concerns · High = likely to hurt admissions chances.
