How Social Studio works
Turn big moments — acceptances, scholarships, decisions — into short, on-brand posts for TikTok, Instagram, LinkedIn, and more.
  • 1
    Choose platform, goal, and tone.
    Tell LaunchPath AI where you’re posting and what you’re announcing so examples match the moment.
  • 2
    Browse and tap sample posts.
    Pick from LaunchPath AI examples tailored to your platform. Tap a sample to load it into your draft.
  • 3
    Edit the draft to sound like you.
    Adjust details, school names, majors, and emojis so the post feels genuine.
  • 4
    Polish, copy, and share.
    Use Smart Review and Polish Post to tighten the message, then copy and paste into your social app.
TikTok & video branding
For TikTok or short-form video, LaunchPath AI can suggest on-screen text and a small watermark logo in the top-left corner of the video (away from TikTok UI). In a full rollout, students could export templates with the LaunchPath AI logo already applied.
